Trek to Mardi

Mardi Himal, nestled in the Annapurna region of Nepal, is a hidden gem for trekkers seeking serene mountain beauty. With pristine forests, tranquil villages, and close encounters with the Himalayan giants, the Mardi Himal trek offers an off-the-beaten-path adventure. The path leads to the base camp, where trekkers are rewarded with stunning vistas of Machapuchare and Annapurna. It's a trek of serenity and natural grandeur, perfect for those yearning for a quieter, lesser-explored Himalayan journey.

Experience magical city Pokhara

Pokhara, Nepal, is a picturesque paradise nestled in the lap of the Annapurna Himalayas. It's a magnet for adventurers, trekkers, and nature enthusiasts. The serene Phewa Lake reflects the snow-capped peaks, while colorful boats add vibrancy to the scenery. Pokhara offers paragliding, trekking, and thrilling outdoor activities. Lakeside cafes and a laid-back atmosphere make it a perfect retreat to unwind after Himalayan adventures.

Discover Pokhara

Magestic Gosaikunda

Gosaikunda, situated in the Langtang National Park of Nepal, is a sacred alpine lake that captivates trekkers and pilgrims alike. Nestled amid rugged landscapes, it's considered a holy site for Hindus and Buddhists. The journey to Gosaikunda takes you through lush forests, picturesque villages, and rhododendron-filled terrain. The serene, glacier-fed lake and its surrounding peaks offer a spiritual and natural retreat, making it a must-visit destination for those seeking both adventure and religious significance.
